🔒 Privacy & Data
AI Camera Tracking: All motion detection is performed on-device. No video or photo data is ever recorded, stored, or transmitted to our servers.

Data Sync: We use secure Apple/Google authentication to sync your rep counts and leaderboard rank across your devices.

Data Deletion: To request the deletion of your account and fitness data, please email us at the address above with your User ID.
